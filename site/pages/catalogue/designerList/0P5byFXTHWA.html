Santa Claus by Steve And Megumi Biddle

Video Tutorial

2020-12-21

Santa is not delayed by the pandemic! Christmas is the best time of the year. Here is the Origami Santa Claus, or if you would prefer Father Christmas. This origami model of Saint Nicolas is designed by Steve and Megumi Biddle. It's perfect as a Christmas tree ornament or decoration. The model is more of the difficult side. I still hope you will give it a try. Enjoy!
