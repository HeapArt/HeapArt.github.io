Bull by John Montroll

Video Tutorial

2021-01-01

Year 2021 is the year of the Ox. To celebrate, the first tutorial of the year is this origami bull designed by John Montroll. this model is slightly on the difficult side. I would recommend you to start with a large piece of square paper 20 cm x 20 cm (9 in x 9 in). In this tutorial I cut a square sheet out of a letter size paper to fold this origami Cow. Happy 2021 and Enjoy!

Thanks for watching!
Wilson Lee @Heap Art
