Traditional Goldfish

Video Tutorial

2020-06-15

Today we are going to learn how to fold the Traditional Origami Goldfish. I learned how to make this one in my grad 2 origami class. This is an all time favorite because it is a model that really resembles the subject it is trying to portray. When I was young I made a lot of these. Making one is like magically pulling a goldfish out of nowhere. I believe that you will definitely enjoy this one.

Thanks for watching!
