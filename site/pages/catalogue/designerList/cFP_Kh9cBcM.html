Octagonal Box (Lid and Base) by Tomoko Fuse

Video Tutorial

2020-12-26

This Beautiful Octagonal Box is designed by Tokomo Fuse. This model is a modular origami piece. It is compose of 8 sheets of square paper: 4 sheets of paper for the lid and 4 sheets for the base. Slightly Complex in the assembly but a really durable design. Perfect as a jewelry or gift box. give it try! This tutorial includes the lid and the base of the modular origami box. Enjoy!

Thanks for watching!
Wilson Lee @Heap Art
