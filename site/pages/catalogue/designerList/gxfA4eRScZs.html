WXYZ by Tung Ken Lam

Video Tutorial

2020-08-01

Today we are going to learn how to fold WXYZ by Tung Ken Lam. This is an amazing model. It is a kusudama star with 4 intersecting triangles. As I am an Engineer, therefore part of me is a mathematician, the mathematician is screaming with joy. This model is a physical model of 4 intersecting planes in 3 dimensional space. I hope you enjoy it as mush as I do.

To make this model you will need 12 pieces of paper to make 12 modules. Ideally 4 colors for the 4 different planes. Therefore 3 modules for each color.
