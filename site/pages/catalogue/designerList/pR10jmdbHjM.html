Space Shuttle by Toshikazu Kawasaki

Video Tutorial

2020-08-27

This is the Space Shuttle aka Orbiter design by Toshikazu Kawasaki. The orbiter was one of my favorite space craft growing up. I've actually seen and touch the orbiter on the NASA Kennedy Launch site . When I saw this model, I really wanted to learn how to fold it and share with you on YouTube.

Thanks for watching!
