Manta Jet by Michael G. LaFosse

Video Tutorial

2021-01-26

This plane is model after an extremely cool fish, the Manta Ray. This is the Manta Jet designed by Michael G. LaFosse. Due to the complexity of the wings, you will need to adjust it a bit when flying the plane. However, it just looks really nice. This plane is another origami plane, folded from a square sheet of paper. I hope you enjoy it!

If you missed my last Exotic Paper Airplane "Paper Plane 037 : Phoenix Plane by Andrew Dewar", you could watch it at https://youtu.be/PNPSHJlrH4Q
