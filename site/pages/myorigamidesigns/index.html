My Designs

Welcome, on this page you'll find a list of my origami designs. Some are simply updates to existing models.

My design philosophy is "Is the existing design good enough? Am I able to do better? If I'm able to better I'll give it a try" Sometime the new design just happens.

Each of these took a significant amount of time to design. Hope you like them.
